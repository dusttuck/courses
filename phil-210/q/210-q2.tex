I've begun each of the following truth tables. Complete them by filling in the missing Ts and Fs.

\problems
\problem{1} (20 points.)
\truthtable
 A | B : A.  ^  .-.B
+++++++++++++++++++++
 T | T : t. *F  .F.t
 T | F : t. *T  .T.f
 F | T : f. *   .F.t
 F | F : f. *   .T.f
\endtruthtable

\problem{2} (15 points.)
\truthtable
 A | B : -.A.  v  .B
+++++++++++++++++++++
 T | T :  .t. *   .t
 T | F :  .t. *   .f
 F | T :  .f. *   .t
 F | F :  .f. *   .f
\endtruthtable

\problem{3} (15 points.)
\truthtable
 A | B : -.[.A. v .B.]
+++++++++++++++++++++++
 T | T :* .[.t.   .t.]
 T | F :* .[.t.   .f.]
 F | T :* .[.f.   .t.]
 F | F :* .[.f.   .f.]
\endtruthtable

\problem{4} (15 points.)
\truthtable
 A | B | C : [.A. v .B.].  ^  .C
+++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++
 T | T | T : [.t.   .t.]. *   .t
 T | T | F : [.t.   .t.]. *   .f
 T | F | T : [.t.   .f.]. *   .t
 T | F | F : [.t.   .f.]. *   .f
 F | T | T : [.f.   .t.]. *   .t
 F | T | F : [.f.   .t.]. *   .f
 F | F | T : [.f.   .f.]. *   .t
 F | F | F : [.f.   .f.]. *   .f
\endtruthtable

\endproblems

Construct truth tables for the following sentences. For each one, state whether it's a tautology, a contradiction, or neither.
